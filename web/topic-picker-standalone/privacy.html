Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 11, 2025

MSS Studio ("MSS", "we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our AI-powered video creation platform.

By using MSS, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

We collect information that you provide directly to us:

  • Account Information: Email address, username, password (hashed)
  • Payment Information: Processed securely through Stripe (we do not store card numbers)
  • Profile Data: Subscription tier, usage statistics

1.2 Content You Create

  • Video scripts and titles you generate
  • AI avatars and logos you create
  • Videos you render and export
  • Usage patterns and preferences

1.3 Automatically Collected Information

  • Log Data: IP address, browser type, device information
  • Cookies: Session cookies for authentication
  • Usage Data: Features used, time spent, errors encountered
  • Analytics: Anonymized usage patterns for service improvement

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information for:

2.1 Service Delivery

  • Creating and managing your account
  • Processing video generation requests
  • Tracking usage limits based on your subscription tier
  • Providing customer support

2.2 Payment Processing

  • Processing subscription payments via Stripe
  • Managing billing and invoices
  • Detecting and preventing fraud

2.3 Service Improvement

  • Analyzing usage patterns to improve features
  • Fixing bugs and technical issues
  • Developing new features
  • Training AI models (using anonymized data only)

2.4 Communication

  • Sending transactional emails (password resets, receipts)
  • Notifying you of service updates or security issues
  • Responding to your inquiries
  • Marketing communications (with your consent, opt-out anytime)

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

3.1 Third-Party Service Providers

We share data with trusted service providers who help us operate MSS:

Provider Purpose Data Shared
Stripe Payment processing Email, payment method
OpenAI AI content generation Your prompts and scripts
Google Cloud Text-to-speech Your scripts for narration
Shotstack Video rendering Your video compositions

3.2 We Do NOT Sell Your Data

We never sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

3.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law or to:

  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Protect our rights and property
  • Prevent fraud or abuse
  • Protect user safety

4. Data Retention

4.1 Account Data

  • Retained while your account is active
  • Deleted within 90 days of account closure
  • Billing records kept for 7 years (tax/legal requirements)

4.2 Video Files

  • Stored for 30 days after creation
  • Automatically deleted after 30 days
  • Download videos to keep them permanently

4.3 Log Data

  • Kept for 90 days for troubleshooting
  • Anonymized analytics kept indefinitely

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • Encryption: HTTPS/TLS for data in transit
  • Password Security: Hashed with SHA-256 (recommend upgrading to bcrypt)
  • Session Management: Secure, httponly cookies
  • Access Controls: Strict internal access policies
  • Regular Audits: Security reviews and updates

Note: No method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Your Rights and Choices

6.1 Access and Portability

  • View your account data in the dashboard
  • Request a copy of your data (contact support)
  • Export your videos at any time

6.2 Correction and Deletion

  • Update your email and username in account settings
  • Request account deletion (contact support)
  • Right to be forgotten (GDPR compliance)

6.3 Marketing Opt-Out

  • Unsubscribe from marketing emails (link in footer)
  • Transactional emails cannot be opted out

6.4 Cookie Control

  • Essential cookies required for service
  • Analytics cookies can be disabled in browser
  • Disabling cookies may limit functionality

7. Cookies and Tracking

7.1 Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential: Session authentication (required)
  • Functional: User preferences and settings
  • Analytics: Usage statistics (anonymized)

7.2 Third-Party Cookies

We do not use third-party advertising cookies. Analytics are self-hosted or minimal.

8. Children's Privacy

MSS is not intended for users under 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we discover we've collected data from a child, we will delete it immediately.

9. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. By using MSS, you consent to such transfers. We ensure adequate safeguards through:

  • Standard contractual clauses
  • Privacy Shield frameworks (where applicable)
  • Service providers' compliance with GDPR/CCPA

10. Your Privacy Rights by Region

10.1 European Users (GDPR)

If you are in the EU/EEA, you have rights to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Rectify inaccurate data
  • Erase your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Restrict processing
  • Data portability
  • Object to processing
  • Lodge a complaint with your supervisory authority

10.2 California Users (CCPA)

California residents have rights to:

  • Know what personal information is collected
  • Know if we sell or disclose personal information (we don't)
  • Opt-out of sale of personal information (we don't sell)
  • Request deletion of personal information
  • Non-discrimination for exercising rights

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of changes by:

  • Updating the "Last Updated" date
  • Sending an email notification for material changes
  • Displaying an in-app notice

Your continued use after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
